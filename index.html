Don't pray for an Easy life

Pray for the strength to endure a hard one

We are in an age where technology is increasing at such an exponential rate and providing us with opportunities and capabilities we've never had before. My passion is finding ways to leverage these technologies to increase not only work flow efficiency but the quality of life. Even as solutions are found to remedy issues, the fluid nature of technology requires innovation stay in the plans. That's where my passion comes from...as a developer I get the opportunity to create and/or innovate. Regardless I find joy in that type of growth and I hope thats a joy I can share with you!
